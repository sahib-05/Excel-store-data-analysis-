## Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

Order ID

Order Date & Month

Sales Amount

Customer Gender & Age Group

Order Status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)

Sales Channels (Amazon, Myntra, Flipkart, Ajio, etc.)

Product Categories

## Dashboard Features

The Excel dashboard provides insights on:

Orders vs Sales Trend (Monthly)

Sales Distribution: Men vs Women

Order Status Breakdown

Orders by Sales Channel

Orders by Age Group & Gender

Interactive Filters (Slicers):

Month

Channel

Category

## Key Insights

Women customers contribute a higher share of total sales.

Most orders are successfully delivered (very low cancellation/refund rate).

Amazon and Myntra are the top-performing sales channels.

Adults form the largest customer segment.

Sales show noticeable monthly fluctuations, helping identify peak periods.

## Business Value

Helps stakeholders track store performance at a glance

Identifies top sales channels and customer segments

Supports data-driven decisions for marketing and inventory planning
